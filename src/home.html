How to Use TricycleFare

1. Select the passenger number tab (1–6) at the top.

2. Enter your From and To locations using the search boxes or by clicking on the map.

3. Press Calculate Fare to compute the route distance.

4. Choose passenger types in the Fare Estimator section to add them and see the total cost.

Fare Rates

• Base Fare: ₱15 (flat rate inside the Base Zone)

• Extended Zone: ₱15 + ₱2/km beyond the base zone

• HS / College discount: −₱3 per passenger

• Elementary discount: −₱5 per passenger

• Daycare / Kinder discount: −₱7 per passenger

Contact

For questions or feedback, reach out to the Koronadal City Transportation Office or use the official TricycleFare feedback form.

About TricycleFare

TricycleFare v1.0

A smart tricycle fare calculator for the City of Koronadal, South Cotabato, Philippines.

Purpose

TricycleFare helps commuters quickly and accurately estimate tricycle fares based on official city ordinance rates, distance, and passenger type discounts.

Technology

Built with Leaflet.js for mapping, OpenStreetMap for geocoding, Leaflet Routing Machine for route drawing, and Turf.js for zone detection.

Disclaimer

Fare estimates are for guidance only and are based on official rate ordinances. Actual fares may vary. Always confirm with your driver.
