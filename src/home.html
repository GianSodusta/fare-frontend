How to Use TricycleFare

1. Select the passenger number tab (1–6) at the top.

2. Enter your From and To locations using the search boxes or click on the map.

3. Press Calculate Fare — this computes the route and saves the trip to your history.

4. Choose passenger types in the Fare Estimator section to see the breakdown.

Fare Rates

• Base Fare: ₱15 (flat rate inside the Base Zone)

• Extended Zone: ₱15 + ₱2/km beyond the base zone

• HS / College discount: −₱3 per passenger

• Elementary discount: −₱5 per passenger

• Daycare / Kinder discount: −₱7 per passenger

Contact

For questions, reach out to the Koronadal City Transportation Office.

About TricycleFare

TricycleFare v1.0

A smart tricycle fare calculator for the City of Koronadal, South Cotabato, Philippines.

Purpose

Helps commuters accurately estimate tricycle fares based on official city ordinance rates, distance, and passenger type discounts.

Technology

Built with Leaflet.js, OpenStreetMap, Leaflet Routing Machine, and Turf.js for zone detection.

Disclaimer

Fare estimates are for guidance only. Always confirm with your driver.
