About the System

TrikeFare is an official fare calculation and management system for tricycle transportation in the City of Koronadal, South Cotabato, Philippines.


The system provides real-time fare estimates based on official rates set by local transport authorities, helping both passengers and drivers ensure fair and transparent pricing across all routes.

How Fares Are Calculated
🏙️
Inside Base Zone
Trips entirely within the base zone are charged a flat base fare regardless of distance
📏
Outside Base Zone
Base fare + per-kilometer rate for trips crossing or outside the zone boundary
🎓
Student Discounts
Discounts applied to HS/College, Elementary, and Daycare/Kinder passengers
Features

Interactive Map

⚡
